Passwords

The use of a unique combination of alpha numeric characters used to unlock access, the more complex and rare a password combination, the harder for attackers to gain admission.

How passwords secure our information systems

We are always told to keep our passwords safe but why

Protects our Account information

By having a secure password, it reduces the chances of an attacker potentially gaining access to sensitive information, making sure you do not have the same password for every account login.

Helps Prevent Future Incoveniences

At a certain point, everyone forgets their passwords, but by keeping note of your password, it can be very useful particulary in sensitive accounts like bank accounts, not having to worry about going through many steps and processes to regain or change your password.

Prevents Password Leak from a Website Attack

There are many instances and cases of attackers hacking or compromising websites where it leaks many users passwords and emails via brute force attack and revealing common passowrds, having a secure password will greatly reduce the chances of this happening.
