Social and Ethical Issues

Social and ethical issues are very important to our private lives, as the wrong person can gain access to sensitive material, which is why we have many computer laws implemented to protect us from our information being used illegally or unethically. Here we will talk about how our personal and sensitive information is kept safe and numerous social problems with online activity and their corresponding solutions.

Mitigation

It is very important to know how we can protect ourselves in the best possible way of intruders and unwanted malware or viruses. Here we will discuss network security, what tools we can use to further protect our information and how we can prevent unwanted traffic flow into our network, what a VPN is and how we can use it to hide our IP address from outside attackers while explaining the benefits of using a VPN.
